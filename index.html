Write books that people will buy because they have already paid for it. Its called serial fiction and it has a long and worthy history. Classics like Ivanhoe by Sir Walter Scott and Tom Wolfe's The Bonfire of the Vanities were published in serial format. Writers can suggest ideas for books, readers can vote with bitcoins on what they want to read and provide feedback as the chapters are written.

The landscape of the publishing world is changing. Too many books are being written which seem like rehashes of earlier books. Figments will be a marketplace of ideas to remove barriers between readers and writers. With the success of e-readers, smart phones and tablets, publishing a book no longer involves the overhead of physical publishing, so why do you need a publisher? By selling chapters at a time, you leverage the masses.
